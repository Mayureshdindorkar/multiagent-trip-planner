## Daily Schedule

### Day 1: March 10, 2025 - Arrival in North Goa
* Arrive at Goa International Airport (GOI)
* Take a taxi or bus to the hotel
* Check-in at Hotel Park Pernem
* Explore the nearby area and have dinner at Café de Port by Radisson Resort Goa Baga

### Day 2: March 11, 2025 - Relaxation Day
* Spend the day relaxing on Calangute Beach
* Take a sunset cruise in the evening

### Day 3: March 12, 2025 - Cultural Experience
* Visit the Se Cathedral in Old Goa
* Explore the Basilica of Bom Jesus and the Chapel of St. Francis of Assisi

# 5-Day Travel Itinerary for Jodhpur (December 3 - December 7, 2025)

## Day 1: Arrival and Exploration of Mehrangarh Fort
- **Morning:**
  - **Arrival:** Land at Jodhpur Airport and check into your hotel. Recommended: Stay at "RAAS Jodhpur" for its proximity to the Mehrangarh Fort and excellent service.
  - **Weather:** Expect sunny weather with temperatures around 24°C (75°F).
  - **Packing Suggestion:** Light cotton clothes, sunglasses, and a hat.
  
- **Afternoon:**
  - **Visit Mehrangarh Fort:** Explore the fort's museum and courtyards.
  - **Lunch:** Enjoy local delicacies at "The Stepwell Café," known for its traditional Rajasthani thali.

- **Evening:**
  - **Jaswant Thada:** A serene visit to this marble marvel at sunset.
  - **Dinner:** Rooftop dining at "Indique" with stunning views of the fort.

- **Budget:**
  - **Accommodation:** $150/night
  - **Meals:** $50
  - **Transport:** $20

## Day 2: Umaid Bhawan Palace and Local Markets
- **Morning:**
  - **Weather:** Warm and sunny, around 25°C (77°F).
  - **Visit Umaid Bhawan Palace:** Explore the museum and gardens.
  
- **Afternoon:**
  - **Lunch:** Dine at "Risala" within Umaid Bhawan for a royal experience.
  - **Mandore Gardens:** Stroll through the historical cenotaphs and gardens.

- **Evening:**
  - **Shopping at Sardar Market:** Explore the bustling market for textiles and handicrafts.
  - **Dinner:** Street food exploration at the market.

- **Budget:**
  - **Accommodation:** $150
  - **Meals:** $40
  - **Shopping:** $50
  - **Transport:** $20

## Day 3: Day Trip to Osian Village
- **All Day:**
  - **Weather:** Pleasant, 24°C (75°F) with cool evening breezes.
  - **Camel Safari and Temple Tours:** Explore the ancient temples of Osian and enjoy a desert safari.
  - **Lunch:** Traditional Rajasthani meal in Osian.

- **Evening:**
  - **Return to Jodhpur:** Relax at the hotel.
  - **Dinner:** "Gypsy Restaurant" for authentic vegetarian cuisine.

- **Budget:**
  - **Day Trip:** $100
  - **Meals:** $40
  - **Transport:** $30

## Day 4: Nature and Relaxation
- **Morning:**
  - **Weather:** Sunny, around 25°C (77°F).
  - **Rao Jodha Desert Rock Park:** Enjoy a nature trail and learn about native flora.
  
- **Afternoon:**
  - **Lunch:** "Cafe Sheesh Mahal" known for its ambiance and local dishes.
  - **Relaxation:** Spend the afternoon in a local café or spa.

- **Evening:**
  - **Dinner:** "On The Rocks" for a unique dining experience amidst nature.

- **Budget:**
  - **Accommodation:** $150
  - **Meals:** $50
  - **Activities:** $20
  - **Transport:** $20

## Day 5: Cultural Immersion and Departure
- **Morning:**
  - **Weather:** Comfortable, around 24°C (75°F).
  - **Local Festivals or Events:** Participate in any ongoing cultural events like the Avataran Divas Mahotsav.
  
- **Afternoon:**
  - **Lunch:** Traditional Rajasthani thali at "Jharokha."

- **Evening:**
  - **Farewell Dinner:** "Hanwant Mahal" for a grand end to your trip.
  - **Departure:** Head to Jodhpur Airport for your return flight.

- **Budget:**
  - **Meals:** $60
  - **Transport:** $20
  - **Miscellaneous:** $30

## Packing Suggestions
- **Clothing:** Light layers for daytime, warmer layers for evenings.
- **Essentials:** Sunglasses, hat, sunscreen, and comfortable walking shoes.
- **Cultural Considerations:** Modest clothing for temple visits.
